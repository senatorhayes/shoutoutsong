Content Policy & Safe Use Guidelines

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

ShoutoutSong is designed for creating fun, personalized songs. This policy outlines acceptable use, content guidelines, and important disclaimers about AI-generated content.

1. Core Principles

ShoutoutSong should be used to:

  • ✅ Create fun, positive, celebratory songs
  • ✅ Surprise and delight friends and family
  • ✅ Mark special occasions and milestones
  • ✅ Express appreciation, love, or friendship
  • ✅ Make people smile and laugh

2. Prohibited Content

You may NOT use ShoutoutSong to create songs that contain or promote:

Illegal Content:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or content sexualizing minors
  • Instructions for illegal activities
  • Copyright or trademark infringement
  • Fraud or impersonation

Harmful Content:

  • Hate speech targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Violence, threats, or incitement to harm
  • Harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse
  • Self-harm or suicide encouragement
  • Dangerous activities or challenges

Inappropriate Content:

  • Sexually explicit or pornographic content
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Extreme profanity or vulgarity (some mild language may appear in AI output)
  • Drug use glorification

Misleading Content:

  • Misinformation or disinformation campaigns
  • Political manipulation or propaganda
  • Impersonation of public figures for deceptive purposes
  • Medical or legal advice (AI is NOT qualified)

3. Copyright & Original Works

Important: All songs created by ShoutoutSong are original AI-generated works and do NOT contain copyrighted melodies or lyrics from third-party artists.
  • Our AI creates original compositions based on your input
  • Songs do NOT reproduce existing copyrighted music
  • You are fully responsible for how you share or distribute your generated content
  • Do NOT falsely claim AI-generated songs as entirely your own original creation if asked

4. AI Output Disclaimers

CRITICAL DISCLAIMERS - READ CAREFULLY:

AI-Generated Content May Include:

  • Fictionalized or imperfect information - AI creates based on patterns, not facts
  • Unexpected interpretations - AI may misunderstand your input
  • Unintended humor or awkwardness - AI lacks human judgment
  • Grammatical imperfections - AI is not perfect with language
  • Cultural insensitivity - Despite our efforts, AI may produce inappropriate content

We Are NOT Liable For:

  • Emotional reactions to AI-generated lyrics or themes
  • Misinterpretations or offense caused by song content
  • Jokes or references that fall flat or seem inappropriate
  • Inaccuracies in AI-generated content about real people or events
  • How recipients react to songs you share with them

Songs Are NOT:

  • ❌ Medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice
  • ❌ Legal, financial, or professional counsel
  • ❌ Factually accurate biographical information
  • ❌ Suitable for all audiences without review
  • ❌ Guaranteed to match your exact expectations

5. User Responsibilities

When using ShoutoutSong, you agree to:

  • Preview before sharing: Listen to songs before giving them to others
  • Use good judgment: Consider the recipient and context
  • Respect privacy: Only use names/details you have permission to use
  • Take responsibility: You're accountable for how you use and share songs
  • Report issues: Flag problematic content to us immediately

6. Consent & Privacy

By creating a song about someone, you confirm:

  • You have the right to use their name and details
  • You're not violating their privacy or consent
  • The song won't be used to harass or harm them
  • You'll respect their wishes if they object to the song

7. Age Restrictions

  • Under 13: Not permitted to use ShoutoutSong
  • Ages 13-17: Require parental consent
  • 18+: Full access with responsibility for appropriate use

8. Enforcement & Moderation

We reserve the right to:

  • Review reported content
  • Remove songs that violate this policy
  • Suspend or ban users who repeatedly violate guidelines
  • Report illegal content to authorities
  • Refuse service to anyone

9. Reporting Violations

If you encounter a song or user behavior that violates this policy:

  • Email: support@shoutoutsong.com
  • Include: Link to content, description of violation, any relevant context
  • We'll review within 48 hours

10. Early Access Product

ShoutoutSong is an early-access AI product under continuous development. This means:
  • Output quality may vary
  • Features may change without notice
  • Occasional technical issues may occur
  • AI models are continuously being improved
  • Your feedback helps us get better!

11. Fair Use Examples

✅ Good Uses:

  • "A birthday song for my mom Sarah who loves gardening"
  • "A funny song for my friend Jake about his new puppy"
  • "An anniversary song for my wife about our trip to Paris"
  • "A motivational song for my teammate Emma who just ran a marathon"

❌ Bad Uses:

  • "A song mocking [person] for their weight/appearance"
  • "A threatening song for my ex"
  • "A political attack song about [candidate]"
  • "A song with explicit sexual content about a coworker"

12. Liability Disclaimer

By using ShoutoutSong, you acknowledge:

  • AI-generated content is unpredictable and may include errors
  • You are solely responsible for reviewing content before sharing
  • ShoutoutSong is NOT liable for how songs are received or interpreted
  • You will not hold us responsible for offense, misunderstandings, or negative reactions
  • Use is at your own risk for entertainment purposes only

13. Changes to Policy

This policy may be updated as our service evolves. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance. Major changes will be communicated via our website.

14. Contact & Support

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@shoutoutsong.com

Remember:

ShoutoutSong is for spreading joy, not harm. Create songs that make people smile, celebrate special moments, and bring happiness. When in doubt, ask yourself: "Would I want someone to create this song about me?" If the answer is no, don't create it.
